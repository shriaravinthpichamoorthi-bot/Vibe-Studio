How it works

Seven phases.
One complete build plan.

Each phase builds on the last. Every decision saves to a markdown file you can download and resume anytime — even in a different session.

01
Ideation
Type your raw idea in plain English. The AI asks targeted questions, synthesises a product kernel, and gives an honest analysis of your idea's strengths and risks.
02
Feature scoping
Industry-standard features suggested for your product type. Flag what's complex. Decide what ships in v1 and what waits — with a v2/v3 roadmap built in.
03
Agent selection
Pick your coding agent — Cursor, Claude Code, Windsurf, Bolt, Lovable, v0. This changes how the build is chunked and how every single prompt is formatted.
04
Stack & architecture
A recommended tech stack specific to your product, explained in plain English. Override anything — we'll tell you the trade-offs honestly.
05
Build chunking
Your product broken into ordered, dependency-aware chunks. Each one is a single agent session with a clear definition of done. Nothing built on a shaky foundation.
06
Prompt generation
Every chunk becomes a fully-formed, agent-ready prompt. Context, constraints, technical spec, done-when checklist — all included. Copy, paste, build.
07
Launch checklist
A deployment checklist specific to your stack and features — not generic advice. Required items flagged. Your v2 roadmap surfaces at the bottom.

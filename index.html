How it works

Seven phases. One build plan.

Each phase builds on the last. Every decision gets saved to a markdown file you can download and resume anytime — even pick up in a different session.

1
Ideation
Type your raw idea in plain English. The AI asks targeted questions, then synthesises a product kernel and gives an honest analysis of your idea's strengths and risks.
2
Feature scoping
We suggest industry-standard features for your product type, flagging anything that's more complex than it looks. You decide what ships in v1 and what waits for v2.
3
Agent selection
Pick the coding agent you'll use. This changes how your build is chunked, how big each prompt is, and what format it needs. Cursor, Claude Code, Bolt, Lovable and more.
4
Stack & architecture
Get a recommended tech stack specific to your product — with plain-English explanations for non-technical founders and honest trade-offs if you override anything.
5
Build chunking
Your product gets broken into ordered, dependency-aware chunks — each one a single agent session with a clear definition of done. Nothing gets built on a shaky foundation.
6
Prompt generation
Every chunk becomes a fully-formed, agent-ready prompt. Context, constraints, technical spec, and done-when checklist all included. Copy, paste, build. No back and forth.
7
Launch checklist
A deployment checklist specific to your stack and features — not generic advice. Required items are flagged. Your v2 roadmap surfaces at the end to keep momentum going.

What you get

One file. Everything in it.

At the end of each session you download a markdown file. It's your product brief, your build plan, your prompts, and your launch checklist — all in one. Import it anytime to resume.

📋
Product brief
Your product kernel — problem, solution, target user, value prop, scope, and success metric. The north star for every decision downstream.
🗂
Feature list
MVP features with complexity ratings, pressure flags on anything tricky, and a v2/v3 roadmap so nothing gets lost.
🏗
Architecture
Pages, data model, auth flow, and external services — specific to your product and stack, not a generic template.
📦
Build plan
Ordered, dependency-aware chunks with done-when checklists. Each chunk knows what came before and what it enables next.
⚡
Agent prompts
One fully-formed prompt per chunk, formatted for your chosen agent. Copy and paste — nothing to add or clarify.
🚀
Launch checklist
Stack-specific checklist covering environment, database, auth, payments, security, and go-live. Required items flagged clearly.
